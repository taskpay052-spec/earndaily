1. Introduction

TaskPay ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Our Commitment: We believe in complete transparency and give you control over your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

Account Information

Name, email, password, profile picture

Payment Information

PayPal email, bank details, crypto wallet

Task Data

Task completions, survey responses, ratings

Usage Data

Login times, pages visited, device info

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and manage your account and earnings
  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process payments and withdrawals
  • Communicate with you about updates and opportunities
  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in operating our platform
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger or acquisition
  • With Your Consent: When you have given explicit permission
Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie preferences below:

Essential Cookies

Required for platform functionality (login, payments, tasks)

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how users interact with our platform

Marketing Cookies

Used to deliver relevant advertisements

Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for platform functionality.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access

Request a copy of your data

Rectification

Correct inaccurate data

Erasure

Request data deletion

Portability

Transfer your data

Object

Opt-out of processing

Automation

Reject automated decisions

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@taskpay.com

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: AES-256 encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure Transmission: SSL/TLS protocols for all data transfer
  • Access Controls: Strict employee access policies
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments
Security First: We continuously update our security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and maintain your account
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

After account deletion, we retain minimal data for legal compliance for up to 90 days.

9. Children's Privacy

TaskPay is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.
